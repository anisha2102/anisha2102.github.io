Research

My research interest is to build machines that are able to perceive and act on multimodal information in their environments. I am interested in the intersection of the fields of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning. I want to focus on learning representations from various modalities such as vision & language to enable agents to learn new tasks efficiently.
