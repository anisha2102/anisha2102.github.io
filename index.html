About Me

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and the Team Lead for Documents AI at a Silicon Valley based Deep Learning startup, HyperVerge, Inc. My work involves training neural networks for solving impactful real world problems. My research interest lies in representation learning for vision and language with a long term goal of making machines learn better representations of the world around us. I believe this is the key to make machines solve harder open ended problems that humans do not possess the resources to do.

I did my undergrad in Computer Science at Pune Institute of Computer Technology.

I love traveling, binge watching and playing the Ukulele! :)

I am a strong supporter of Inclusive AI. If you identify yourself as a minority in the field of AI and want to discuss more, feel free to reach out. :)
